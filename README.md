# Stevey, Mapped — A Field Map of Steve Yegge

A small, dark, static field map of [Steve Yegge](https://steve-yegge.medium.com/)'s writing — the long, funny, opinionated essays engineers actually pass around, from the **Google Platforms Rant** and the **Kingdom of Nouns** to **Cheating Is All You Need** and **The Flat Curve Society**.

One page per essay, the original pinned at the top, the best lines quoted verbatim and linked back to their source.

**Live:** https://idvorkin-ai-tools.github.io/yegge-field-map/

## What's here

- **The classic rants** (Amazon & Google years): Platforms Rant · Kingdom of Nouns · Mystery Machine Bus · Get That Job at Google · Tour de Babel
- **The AI era**: Cheating Is All You Need · The Junior Developer, Dead & Reborn (CHOP) · Gas Town & the Heresies · The Flat Curve Society · Vibe Coding (the book)
- **[The Quotable Yegge](quotes.html)** — a verified quote collection, each line linked to its source

## Notes

Not affiliated with Steve Yegge. Summaries and framing are ours; every quote is his, verified against the original (or an archived mirror where the original is offline) and linked back. If a page misreads an essay, the original always wins — go read it.
